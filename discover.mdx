## Signals

Discover uses 13 signals, grouped into two tiers based on typical risk profile. A token can qualify for multiple signals at once — more signals strengthens the case, but does not mean lower risk.

### Lower-Risk Signals

| Signal | What It Flags |
| --- | --- |
| **Safe Bets** | Established tokens with deep liquidity, strong holder counts, and proven track records. |
| **Growth** | Tokens with consistent positive momentum across price, holders, and volume over time. |
| **Vetted** | Tokens that pass automated checks: verified contract, no honeypot signals, locked liquidity. |
| **Inst. Radar** | Tokens being accumulated by institutional or smart-money wallets. |
| **Recovery** | Quality tokens that have bounced from local lows with renewed buying interest. |

### Higher-Risk Signals

| Signal | What It Flags |
| --- | --- |
| **Ignition** | Early breakout signs: volume surge, holder growth, on-chain accumulation in a short window. |
| **Viral** | Tokens trending across social platforms with rapidly growing mentions and engagement. |
| **LP Lock** | New launches where liquidity is locked — a basic anti-rug indicator on otherwise unproven tokens. |
| **Alpha** | Under-the-radar tokens with unusual smart-money activity before broader market awareness. |
| **Diverge** | Tokens where on-chain activity diverges from price action — quiet accumulation, or distribution into rallies. |
| **Revival** | Older, dormant tokens seeing renewed activity and volume after extended periods of inactivity. |
| **Social** | Tokens picking up specifically on Twitter and Farcaster among crypto-native accounts. |
| **Contrarian** | Tokens where retail and smart-money flows are moving in opposite directions. |

## Buying & Selling

All buys and sells from Discover route through Fere's gasless execution layer. You don't need native gas tokens on any chain — Fere covers gas and settles in the token you're trading.

**Supported on Discover:** Solana, Base, Ethereum, Arbitrum, BNB Chain, Polygon.

<Tip>
  **Slippage, Routing, Confirmation.** Slippage, priority fees, and routing are handled automatically. You see the quoted price before confirming and can cancel up to the moment of submission.
</Tip>

## Filters

Filters narrow the list to tokens flagged with a specific signal. Multiple filters stack — selecting **Safe Bets** and **Growth** together shows only tokens that hit both, which tends to return a small, high-conviction list. Removing all filters shows every flagged token across all signals.

## FAQ

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="How often does Discover update?">
    Continuously. The "Updated Ns ago" indicator at the top right shows the last refresh — new signals can appear within seconds of meeting our thresholds.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Why is a token I expected not showing up?">
    A token only appears if it currently meets the threshold for at least one of the 13 signals. Tokens that previously trended may have rolled off if their momentum or smart-money interest faded.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="What's the difference between Discover and Market Pulse?">
    Market Pulse summarizes overall market chatter and narratives. Discover surfaces specific tokens to consider. Pulse tells you what the market is talking about; Discover tells you which tokens are worth looking at right now.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Is Discover financial advice?">
    No. Every signal is a data-driven flag, not a recommendation. Risk badges are heuristics, not guarantees. Always do your own research before trading, and never trade more than you can afford to lose.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can I trade tokens not in Discover?">
    Yes — use the Agent to research and trade any token directly by name or contract address, on any of our six supported chains.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
