You don't need to think about chains, bridges, or gas tokens. Tell Fere what you want to buy — it figures out where your money is and how to get it there.

## Supported Chains

- **Ethereum**
- **Base**
- **Arbitrum**
- **BNB Chain**
- **Polygon**
- **Solana**

You'll see all supported chains at the bottom of your wallet panel. New chains are added regularly.

<Note>
  **Hyperliquid** is also supported for **perpetual futures** trading. See [Hyperliquid](/hyperliquid) for perp setup, funding, and withdrawals.
</Note>

## How It Works

You don't say *"bridge ETH from Mainnet to Base."* You say what you actually want:

> **Cross-Chain Example**
>
> *"I want to buy AERO on Base using my Mainnet ETH"*

**Fere automatically:** detects you lack funds on Base, finds your ETH on Mainnet, selects the optimal bridge, bundles the bridge and swap into one flow, estimates time (~2 min), and asks for confirmation before executing.

## Gasless by Default

One of Fere's most loved features. You don't need to hold native gas tokens on every chain you want to trade on. Fere handles gas abstraction through the CDP wallet infrastructure — meaning you can trade on Base, Arbitrum, or BNB Chain without first buying and bridging gas tokens.

## Cross-Chain in Action

Example instruction:

```
Bridge my Polygon holdings to Base and swap everything to USDC
```

**Agent execution log:**

1. Parsing intent: cross-chain bridge + swap
2. Scanning Polygon wallet
3. Found: 15.2 POL ($7.45)
4. Created plan with 3 task(s)
5. Task 1: Get POL token address on Polygon — resolved
6. Task 2: Get USDC address on Base — resolved
7. Task 3: Bridge & swap POL → USDC via Across
8. Bridging in progress... ETA ~90 seconds
9. Bridge complete. Swapping to USDC
10. 7.29 USDC received on Base
11. All tasks complete

<Check>
  **Bridge Times.** Fere always estimates completion time before you confirm. Typical ranges: 30 seconds (Base ↔ Arbitrum) to ~5 minutes (Ethereum → Solana). The agent picks the fastest reliable route automatically.
</Check>
