Invite friends to Fere and earn credits when they complete eligible credit-pack spend. It's one of the easiest ways to keep your agent fueled.

## How It Works

Open **Refer and Earn** from the **profile menu** (top-right avatar → **Refer and Earn**). You'll see your unique referral codes — short alphanumeric codes you can share with anyone.

You earn **200 credits** plus **5 points** when someone you referred completes **$10 cumulative** eligible spend on **credit packs** — for example on-chain wallet top-ups or card / Commerce checkouts, where those options are available in your app. **Subscription** charges and **trading fees** do not count toward this $10 threshold.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Copy Your Code">
    You get multiple unique referral codes. Copy any one using the clipboard button next to it.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Share It">
    Send the code to friends, post it on social media, share it in your group chats — wherever you like.
  </Step>
  <Step title="They Sign Up & Spend on Credit Packs">
    Your friend creates a Fere account with your referral code, then reaches **$10 cumulative** eligible credit-pack spend (tracked across qualifying top-ups).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Rewards Unlock">
    When the threshold is met, you receive **200 credits** and **5 points**. Your friend receives **5 points** for activating.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Info>
  **Referral Credit Expiry.** Each batch of referral credits expires **14 days after it is granted** if you (the referrer) have **no completed agent queries** in that window. Signup credits and purchased credits follow different rules — see [Credits](/credits).
</Info>

<Tip>
  **Lifetime Cap.** Referral **credit** rewards are capped at **10 successful referrals** — **2,000 credits** total at 200 credits each. After you hit the cap, successful referrals still earn you **5 points** each toward the weekly leaderboard.
</Tip>

<Info>
  **Referral Leaderboard.** Refer and Earn shows a **leaderboard ranked by referral activity**. Top referrers are displayed publicly — compete for the top spot.
</Info>
