Your wallet is always visible on the right side of the screen — showing your balance, assets, and active orders in real time. Under the hood, it's powered by Coinbase's most advanced wallet infrastructure.

## What You See

The wallet panel is always visible on the right side of the Fere interface. At a glance, you can see:

- **Total balance** — Your aggregate holdings across all chains.
- **Wallet addresses** — Both your EVM address (for Base, Ethereum, Polygon, etc.) and your Solana address, each with a key icon to export your private key.
- **Add Funds** — One-click deposit into your wallet.
- **Assets tab** — Your token holdings with USD values, organized by chain. A "Hide dust" checkbox filters out tiny balances under $0.05.
- **Limit Orders tab** — All your active limit orders in one place.
- **TP/SL Orders** — Active take-profit and stop-loss positions appear right under the relevant token. Cross-chain buys arm TP/SL against the destination-chain wallet that holds the tokens.
- **Supported chains** — Shown at the bottom of the wallet panel.

### Strategy wallets

Day, Swing, and other automated strategies each get a **dedicated wallet** funded from your main wallet. In the panel you will see:

- **Per-strategy cards** — Balance and holdings for each active strategy, with **Add funds** and **Withdraw** (spot tokens back to your main wallet; the strategy stays active).
- **Archived strategies** — Closed or paused-away strategies that still hold a balance. Withdraw remaining funds here; the card disappears once empty.

Pausing a strategy in Settings stops new checks but does **not** auto-sweep funds — use **Withdraw** or delete the strategy to return capital to your main wallet. See [Hyperliquid → Automated Strategies](/hyperliquid#automated-strategies) for Day/Swing details.

Specialized balances also appear here when you use integrated products:

- **Polymarket** — pUSD cash and open prediction-market positions. See [Polymarket](/polymarket).
- **Hyperliquid** — **USDC (Hyperliquid)** collateral and open perp positions. Use **Sell** on USDC to withdraw, or on a perp row to close. See [Hyperliquid](/hyperliquid).

<Info icon="lock">
  **Powered by Coinbase CDP Server Wallets.** Every Fere wallet is a Coinbase Developer Platform Server Wallet — the same infrastructure trusted by the world's largest crypto exchange. Private keys live inside Trusted Execution Environments (TEEs) and never leave the secure enclave.
</Info>

## You Own Your Wallet

This is critical: **your wallet is fully owned by you.** Next to each wallet address in the panel, you'll see a key icon. Click it to reveal and export your private key. You can then import that key into any external wallet like MetaMask or Phantom — meaning your funds are never locked inside Fere.

<Check>
  **Full Key Ownership.** Your private key is yours. Export it anytime and use it in MetaMask (for EVM chains) or Phantom (for Solana). Fere is non-custodial — you can always access your funds independently. See the [FAQ](/faq) for step-by-step export instructions.
</Check>

## What Makes This Secure

- **One wallet, every chain** — A single wallet identity works across all supported EVM chains and Solana.
- **Keys never exposed** — Private keys are generated and stored inside hardware-isolated enclaves. Not even Coinbase or Fere can access them.
- **Policy enforcement** — Transaction rules like spend limits and address allowlists are enforced at the enclave level.
- **Sub-200ms signing** — Wallet creation takes under 500ms. Transaction signing under 200ms.
- **Non-custodial** — Fere cannot seize, freeze, or move your funds. Period.

**Infrastructure:** Coinbase CDP · Trusted Execution (TEE) · AWS Nitro Enclaves
