## Setup — One Click, Once

Polymarket requires a dedicated Safe wallet on Polygon to trade. Fere creates and manages it for you.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Add at least $10 to your Fere wallet">
    Polymarket trading unlocks once your total Fere portfolio crosses $10 — across any chain, any token. If you're below that, Fere prompts you to add funds first (any chain, any token, all gasless).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Enable Trading">
    Fere deploys your Polymarket Safe on Polygon, signs the required CLOB approvals, and stores the trading keys — all in one async task. Takes about 30 seconds.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Fund your Polymarket Safe">
    Pick any token on any supported chain. Fere bridges → swaps → wraps into pUSD on Polygon and deposits it into your Safe. Gasless, single confirmation.
  </Step>
</Steps>

Your Polymarket Safe is self-custodial. The keys live in your Fere wallet — exportable from the wallet panel anytime.

<Tip>
  **Take profit is the unlock.** Polymarket has no native stop-loss or take-profit. Most traders babysit positions and exit too early or too late. With Fere, you set it once at order time and walk away — Fere handles the watch and the exit. It's the single biggest reason users prefer trading Polymarket here.
</Tip>

**Order minimums:** $1 minimum order size. CLOB price range $0.001–$0.999. Anything outside that range is rejected by Polymarket itself.

## Funding — Gasless, From Any Chain

Polymarket trades in pUSD on Polygon. You almost certainly don't have pUSD on Polygon. That's fine — Fere does the conversion.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Add Funds from the Polymarket page">
    Tap the funding banner or any "Bridge funds" CTA. The modal opens with your full Fere balance across every chain visible.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Pick any token on any supported chain">
    Ethereum, Base, Arbitrum, Polygon, BNB Chain, Solana — any token Fere can swap. ETH, SOL, USDC, USDT, your random meme bag — all eligible.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter a USD amount">
    Fere shows the equivalent in your selected token at live prices. The deposit completes once the cross-chain route lands on Polygon and wraps into pUSD inside your Safe.
  </Step>
</Steps>

You never pay gas. You never sign a Polygon transaction. You never see USDC.e or pUSD as separate balances — Fere unifies them under your Polymarket cash holding.
