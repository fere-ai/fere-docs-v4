## What Is This?

Polymarket is the leading prediction market platform — you bet on real-world outcomes. But finding the right markets to trade is hard. There are thousands of markets, and the homepage algorithm isn't great at surfacing the ones with the best risk-reward opportunities.

Fere solves this. An AI agent constantly analyzes active Polymarket markets — evaluating odds, volume, time to expiry, news sentiment, and historical patterns — to recommend the markets most worth your attention.
