Subscriptions give you a **recurring monthly credit allowance** on top of everything else Fere offers. You do not need a subscription to use the platform — signup bonus, referrals, and [credit packs](/credits) all work without one — but a plan is the best value if you chat, run strategies, or trade regularly.

## Why Subscribe?

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Monthly credit allowance" icon="coins">
    Each plan grants a fixed batch of credits **every month** — Lite, Pro, and Whale scale from casual use to power-user volume. Credits land automatically when your billing cycle renews.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Better value than one-off packs" icon="chart-line">
    Subscription plans include **more credits per dollar** than buying individual [credit packs](/credits). Higher tiers multiply that advantage further.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Subscriber top-up bonus" icon="gift">
    Active subscribers get an extra **bonus percent** on every credit top-up from the pricing page — useful when you burn through your monthly allocation mid-cycle.
  </Card>
  <Card title="2× points on subscription spend" icon="trophy">
    Every subscription payment earns **2× points per USD** spent (see [Points & Leaderboard](/points)). Top-ups and on-chain activity earn points separately.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

<Tip>
  **On-chain execution stays free.** Successful swaps, bridges, transfers, and Polymarket orders do not consume chat credits after execution completes — you still need enough credits to **start** the agent turn, but verified on-chain actions are waived.
</Tip>

## Plans

Fere offers three tiers. Prices are shown live on the [**Pricing**](https://fereai.xyz/pricing) page (billed in USD via Stripe). Credit grants below are **per month** — even on annual billing, credits accrue monthly.

| Tier | Credits / month | Best for |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Lite** | 1,100 | Regular chat, light research, occasional strategies |
| **Pro** | 4,200 | Daily agent use, Market Pulse, active Polymarket research |
| **Whale** | 16,000 | Heavy automation, multiple strategies, high-volume trading |

Each plan card on `/pricing` also lists tier-specific feature bullets (pulled from our catalog). **Pro** is marked *Most popular* for new subscribers.

### Monthly vs annual

Toggle **Monthly** or **Annual** at the top of the pricing page. Annual plans bill once per year (typically **~17% less** than paying monthly × 12). Credits still refresh **every month** on annual subscriptions — you do not receive the full year upfront.

## How Subscription Credits Work

Subscription credits live in a **separate bucket** from signup, referral, and purchased credits.

**Spend order** (first drained → last):

1. Signup bonus credits
2. Referral credits
3. **Subscription credits**
4. Purchased / top-up credits

**Monthly refresh — no rollover.** When your billing cycle renews, unused subscription credits from the previous period are replaced by your new monthly grant. You always start the new cycle with a fresh allocation (subject to your tier's balance cap).

**Top-ups survive cancel.** If you cancel, only the subscription bucket is cleared at period end. Any credits you bought via [credit packs](/credits) stay in your account.

**Balance cap.** Each tier caps how many subscription credits you can hold at once (roughly **2× your monthly grant**). Upgrading adds credits immediately; downgrades take effect at the end of the current billing period.

<Info>
  **No free trial.** Subscriptions bill through Stripe Checkout on day one. You can cancel anytime — access continues until the end of the paid period.
</Info>

## How to Subscribe

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Pricing">
    Go to [**/pricing**](https://fereai.xyz/pricing) from the top navigation (**Pricing**) while signed in.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Pick monthly or annual">
    Use the **Monthly / Annual** toggle. Annual shows a savings badge when available.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose a tier">
    Compare Lite, Pro, and Whale. Each card shows the live price, credits per month, and included features.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Subscribe">
    Click **Subscribe** on your chosen plan. You must be signed in with a real email on file. Checkout opens in **Stripe** — pay with card.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Return to Fere">
    After payment, Stripe redirects back to `/pricing`. Fere confirms your subscription and adds credits (usually within seconds). A success banner appears when active.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
  **Wallet-only accounts.** If you signed up with a wallet and have a synthetic `@wallet.fereai` email, add a real email under **Settings** before subscribing so receipts and billing alerts reach you.
</Warning>

## Manage Your Subscription

Once subscribed, the pricing page shows your **current plan**, next billing date, and card on file.

| Action | How |
| --- | --- |
| **Update payment method** | **Manage subscription** → Stripe Billing Portal |
| **Upgrade** | Click **Upgrade to …** on a higher tier — charges immediately; new credits arrive after payment confirms |
| **Downgrade** | Click **Downgrade to …** — scheduled for end of current billing period; no refund for unused time |
| **Switch to annual / monthly** | Same tier, different cadence — annual switches bill now; monthly-from-annual schedules at period end |
| **Cancel** | Billing Portal → cancel at period end — benefits continue until the date shown |
| **Resume** | If cancel is pending, **Resume subscription** on `/pricing` before the end date |
| **Top up mid-cycle** | Scroll to **Top up credits** on `/pricing` for $5 / $10 / $20 packs with your subscriber bonus |

Downgrades and cadence switches that schedule for later can be reversed with **Keep current plan** before they take effect.

## Subscriptions vs Credit Packs

You can use both — they are designed to work together.

| | **Subscription** | **Credit pack (top-up)** |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Billing** | Recurring (monthly or annual) | One-time |
| **Credits** | Fixed monthly grant per tier | $5 → 300, $10 → 650, $20 → 1,400 |
| **Expiry** | Refreshes each cycle (no rollover) | **Never expire** |
| **Best when** | You use Fere every week | You need a one-off boost or prefer pay-as-you-go |
| **Payment** | Card via Stripe Checkout | Card or crypto from **Top up credits** on `/pricing` |

<Note>
  **Market Pulse pricing is separate.** Scheduled Market Pulse runs cost a fixed **40 credits (12h)** or **70 credits (24h)** per run, drawn from whichever credit bucket is next in the spend order.
</Note>
