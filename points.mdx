Points reward your on-chain activity and credit purchases — the more you transact, the more you earn. Climb the leaderboard and build your streak. Points refresh every Monday.

## How Points Work

Points are earned through spend-volume actions: on-chain transactions (swaps, bridges, transfers), Polymarket trades, and credit purchases. Open **Points & Leaderboard** from the left sidebar to see your stats.

## What You See

The Points dashboard gives you a clear snapshot of your standing:

- **Total** — Your all-time accumulated points.
- **Last Week** — Points earned in the previous weekly cycle.
- **Rank** — Your current position among all users (e.g., Top 10%).
- **Streak** — How many consecutive weeks you've been active, shown as a progress bar.

## How to Earn Points

Points are awarded automatically based on your platform activity. Here's exactly how each action is rewarded:

| Activity | Points Earned |
| --- | --- |
| Referral Reward | 5 points per referral you make |
| Referred Bonus | 5 points when you're referred by someone |
| On-Chain Transaction | 1× per USD of transaction value |
| Polymarket Order | 1× per USD of order value |
| Credit Purchase | 1× per USD spent on credits |

<Tip>
  **Deactivated Activities.** Market Pulse and Chat no longer earn points. Historical points from these activities are preserved.
</Tip>

<Info>
  **Weekly Refresh.** Points reset every Monday. This keeps the leaderboard competitive and gives everyone a fresh chance to climb the ranks each week. Your all-time total is tracked separately, so your lifetime contribution is never lost.
</Info>

## Points Breakdown

On the Points page, you'll see a **Points Breakdown** section showing exactly where your points came from — a visual bar chart with categories like On-Chain Transaction, Polymarket, and Credit Purchase. Categories with zero points are automatically hidden. Click the **"How to Earn Points"** button for the full breakdown modal.

## Leaderboard

Below the breakdown, the **Leaderboard** shows the top 30 performers for the current week. Your rank, username (partially masked for privacy), and point total are displayed. If you're in the top positions, you'll see gold, silver, and bronze medals next to your rank.

<Tip>
  **Why Points Matter.** Points are more than just a vanity metric. Active users who consistently rank high on the leaderboard are building a track record of platform engagement — and that will matter as Fere introduces new reward tiers, airdrops, and exclusive features for top contributors. Start building your streak now.
</Tip>
