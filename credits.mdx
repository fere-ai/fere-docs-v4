Every action Fere takes on your behalf costs credits. Think of credits as the fuel that powers your AI agent — you get a free signup bonus, and can purchase credit packs when you need more.

## How Credits Work

Credits are the currency of the Fere platform. Chat queries and most scheduled actions use dynamic pricing based on complexity. Market Pulse is fixed at 70 credits for 24h or 40 credits for 12h. Your current credit balance is always visible in the **top-right corner** of the screen.

| Action | Credit Cost |
| --- | --- |
| Fere AI Agent Query | Dynamic per query (based on complexity) |
| Market Pulse (Scheduled) | Fixed: 70 credits (24h) or 40 credits (12h) |

## Credit Packs

Need more credits? Purchase a credit pack directly with crypto from your Fere wallet. Click **"Add Credits"** from the profile menu (top-right avatar) or the credits display.

| Pack | Credits | Effective Rate |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **\$5** | 300 credits | 60 credits / $ |
| **\$10** | 650 credits | 65 credits / $ |
| **\$20** | 1,400 credits | 70 credits / $ |

<Tip>
  **Bigger Packs, Better Value.** Higher-tier packs give you more credits per dollar. The \$20 pack grants **17% more credits per dollar** than the \$5 pack.
</Tip>

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Add Credits">
    Click your profile avatar (top-right) → "Add Credits" to open the dialog.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose a Pack">
    Select one of the three credit packs: **\$5**, **\$10**, or **\$20**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select Payment Token">
    Choose which chain (Ethereum, Base, Arbitrum, Solana, BNB, Polygon) and token to pay with.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm">
    Review the transaction and confirm. Credits are added to your account once the on-chain transfer completes.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Info>
  **Pay With Any Token.** You're not limited to stablecoins. You can pay using any token on any supported chain — ETH, SOL, USDC, memecoins, anything in your wallet. Fere handles the conversion.
</Info>
