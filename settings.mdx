Open **Settings** from the profile menu — click your avatar in the top-right corner, then **Settings**.

## Email

If you signed up with a wallet, you'll see an **Email Settings** card first. Click **Edit** to add or change the email Fere uses for account notifications and recovery. If your account still has a system-generated email, you'll see a prompt to replace it — do this before [subscribing](/subscriptions) so billing receipts and alerts reach you.

## Privacy

Toggle **Private chats by default** to keep new conversations out of your visible chat history. Private chats are still reachable if you have the direct link — they just won't show up in the **Chats** sidebar.

## Hyperliquid

Shows whether your Hyperliquid trading key is set up and ready. From here you can:

- **Re-run setup** — provision the trading key Fere uses to place perp orders on your behalf.
- **Rotate** — refresh a Fere-managed trading key.
- View every trading key Hyperliquid has on file for your wallet address, including any key not created by Fere.

See [Hyperliquid](/hyperliquid) for the full funding and trading walkthrough.

## Invite Code

If you haven't applied a referral code yet, enter one here to link your account to whoever referred you. Once applied, it's shown read-only. To get your own code to share with friends, use **Refer and Earn** from the profile menu instead — see [Refer & Earn](/refer-and-earn).

## Contact Us

A direct link to reach the Fere team for product questions or support.

## Delete Account

Scroll to the bottom for **Delete account**. This starts a short, three-step confirmation flow — nothing is deleted until you finish all three steps:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Review">
    Fere shows anything that needs to be resolved first — an open billing dispute, an unwithdrawn Polymarket or Hyperliquid balance, open positions, or a pending on-chain transaction. You can't continue until these are cleared. This step also summarizes your wallets and their balances.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Back up your wallet keys">
    Download a backup of your Fere wallet's private keys before you lose access to them. You'll need to confirm you've saved the file and that you understand Fere cannot recover these keys once your account is deleted.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Tell us why">
    Pick a reason you're leaving and optionally add feedback. Then press and hold the confirm button for a couple of seconds to schedule deletion — this is deliberate, so you don't delete your account by accident.
  </Step>
</Steps>

After you confirm, your account deletion is scheduled and you're signed out. If you change your mind before it takes effect, come back to **Settings** and click **Keep account**.

<Warning>
  **Export your keys first.** Once your account is deleted, Fere permanently destroys its copy of your wallet keys. If you didn't download them in Step 2, any remaining funds are unrecoverable.
</Warning>
