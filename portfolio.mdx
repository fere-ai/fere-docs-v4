Portfolio pulls your holdings, profit and loss, and trade history from every venue into a single dashboard. Open **Portfolio** from the navigation to see it.

## What You See

At the top of the page, a hero card shows four numbers at a glance:

- **Total Portfolio Value** — the current USD value of everything you hold, across all venues.
- **Live Position PnL** — unrealized profit or loss on your currently open positions.
- **Total Realized PnL** — profit or loss you've already locked in, across all venues, all-time.
- **Traded Volume** — your cumulative trading volume, all-time.

<Tip>
  **Live vs. Realized.** Live Position PnL moves with the market because it's based on what you still hold. Total Realized PnL only changes when you actually close a position — it won't move just because prices do.
</Tip>

## Your Strategies

Right under the hero, a **Your Strategies** section lists each of your isolated per-strategy wallets — one per trading strategy, separate from your main wallet — with **Fund** and **Withdraw** buttons for each. If any strategy was cancelled while its wallet still held a balance, it appears below as a self-hiding **Archived strategies** group, offering **Withdraw** only. The section shows nothing if you have no active or funded-archived strategies. See [Strategy Wallets](/strategies#strategy-wallets) for the full picture, including funding minimums and the setup checklist.

## By Venue

Below the hero, a row of venue cards breaks your portfolio down by where it's held — including **Tokens** (your regular on-chain holdings), **Polymarket**, and **Hyperliquid**. Click a card to filter everything below it — both **Open Positions** and **Trade History** — down to that venue. Click it again, or click **All Venues**, to clear the filter.

<Info>
  Each venue's PnL is sourced from that venue where possible — Polymarket and Hyperliquid report their own lifetime PnL, while token PnL is calculated from your trade history on Fere. If a venue's data is temporarily unavailable, its PnL shows as `--` instead of a number.
</Info>

## Open Positions & Trade History

Two tabs sit below the venue cards:

- **Open Positions** — everything you currently hold, with the same actions available as in your wallet panel: expand a row for details, **Sell**, **Redeem** a resolved Polymarket position, or manage an attached **TP/SL** order.
- **Trade History** — a chronological log of every trade and transfer, newest first. Each row shows the asset, side, and USD value. An **Activity** tag shows what originated it — a **Strategy** or **Chat** you can click through to, an automated **AI** action (like a scheduled rebalance), a **manual** order, or **off-platform** activity that happened directly on a venue outside of Fere. Scroll down and click **Load more** for older history.

<Note>
  Trade History rows show what happened, not a per-trade profit number — for PnL, use the hero card and venue cards above.
</Note>

Next to the tabs, two checkboxes — **Hide dust** and **Hide unverified** — filter small balances and unverified tokens out of the view. These are the same toggles used in your wallet panel, so changing one updates both.

## Your Wallets

Click the wallet icon next to your total value to open **Your Wallets** — a quick view of your EVM and Solana addresses and their balances. To export a private key, use the key icon in the main wallet panel — see [Wallet & Security](/wallet).

<Tip>
  Portfolio refreshes automatically about once a minute, and whenever you switch back to the tab.
</Tip>
