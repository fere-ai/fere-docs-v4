Portfolio pulls your holdings, profit and loss, and trade history from every venue into a single dashboard. Open **Portfolio** from the navigation to see it. The page has two parts: a summary banner across the top, and an **Accounts** rail on the left that switches the detail pane on the right between your main wallet and each strategy wallet.

## Total Net Worth

A summary banner sits at the top of the page and doesn't change as you switch accounts below it:

- **Total Net Worth** — the combined USD value of your main wallet plus every strategy wallet, with a caption ("Main wallet only", or "Main wallet + N strategies") showing how many accounts feed it.
- **Unrealized today** — combined live position PnL across all of those accounts.
- **Realized PnL** — profit or loss you've already locked in, all-time. This figure covers your **main wallet only** — strategy wallets don't yet report their own realized PnL.
- **Traded Volume** — your cumulative trading volume, all-time, also **main-wallet only**.

Below the numbers, an allocation bar shows how your combined net worth splits across accounts — a segment per account, plus an aggregated **Closed** segment if any strategy was cancelled while still holding a balance.

<Tip>
  **Live vs. Realized.** Unrealized PnL moves with the market because it's based on what you still hold. Realized PnL only changes when you actually close a position — it won't move just because prices do.
</Tip>

## Accounts

The **Accounts** rail lists every wallet feeding your portfolio: **Main wallet** pinned first (marked "Parent account"), then one row per strategy wallet — each a separate, isolated wallet dedicated to a single trading strategy. Any strategy cancelled while its wallet still held a balance appears below in its own **Closed · still funded** group instead. Each row shows that account's balance and live PnL; click a row to inspect it in the pane on the right. On mobile the rail scrolls horizontally above the pane; on desktop it's a sticky left column. See [Strategy Wallets](/strategies#strategy-wallets) for how these wallets get created and funded.

## Main Wallet

Selecting **Main wallet** (the default) shows your parent account in full:

- Balance, and a **venue lens** — a chip for **Tokens** (your regular on-chain holdings), **Polymarket**, and **Hyperliquid**. Click a chip to filter both **Open Positions** and **Trade History** below down to that venue; click it again to clear the filter.
- **Open Positions** — everything you currently hold, with the same actions available as in your wallet panel: expand a row for details, **Sell**, **Redeem** a resolved Polymarket position, or manage an attached **TP/SL** order.
- **Trade History** — a chronological log of every trade and transfer, newest first. Each row shows the asset, side, and USD value. An **Activity** tag shows what originated it — a **Strategy** or **Chat** you can click through to, an automated **AI** action (like a scheduled rebalance), a **manual** order, or **off-platform** activity that happened directly on a venue outside of Fere. Scroll down and click **Load more** for older history.
- Two checkboxes next to the tabs — **Hide dust** and **Hide unverified** — filter small balances and unverified tokens out of the view. These are the same toggles used in your wallet panel, so changing one updates both.
- The wallet-cluster icon next to your balance opens **Your Wallets** — a quick view of your EVM and Solana addresses and their balances. To export a private key, use the key icon in the main wallet panel — see [Wallet & Security](/wallet).

<Note>
  Trade History rows show what happened, not a per-trade profit number — for PnL, use the summary banner and venue lens above.
</Note>

## A Strategy Wallet

Selecting a strategy row instead shows that strategy's own isolated wallet:

- Balance, its EVM and/or Solana address (each with a copy button), and **Fund** / **Withdraw** buttons. **Fund** doesn't appear for a closed strategy — only **Withdraw** does.
- The same **venue lens**, built from that strategy's own holdings, filtering the positions list below it.
- A read-only **Positions** list. Strategies trade automatically from their own wallet, so there's no **Sell** or **TP/SL** here, and no separate trade history for a single strategy — use Main Wallet's Trade History for a cross-account activity feed instead.

<Info>
  Each venue's PnL is sourced from that venue where possible — Polymarket and Hyperliquid report their own lifetime PnL, while token PnL is calculated from your trade history on Fere. If a venue's data is temporarily unavailable, its PnL shows as `--` instead of a number.
</Info>

<Tip>
  Portfolio refreshes automatically about once a minute, and whenever you switch back to the tab.
</Tip>
