Fere scans millions of messages across every major crypto channel — Twitter/X, Telegram, Discord, Farcaster, Reddit, Signal, and news outlets — and distills it into one beautiful, actionable summary.

## How It Works

Open **Market Pulse** from the left sidebar, choose a time window — say, the last 8 hours — and hit generate. Within seconds, Fere's agents comb through the entire fire hose of crypto social media and deliver a structured report covering everything that happened while you were away.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Choose Your Window">
    Select how far back you want to look — 4 hours, 8 hours, 24 hours, or custom.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Fere Scans Everything">
    Millions of messages across Twitter/X, Telegram, Discord, Farcaster, Reddit, Signal, and news — analyzed by AI in real time.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Get Your Digest">
    A beautifully structured report with key narratives, alpha calls, whale movements, on-chain signals, and degen plays.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Sample Digest

A typical Market Pulse report includes:

- **Key Narratives** — The dominant themes of the window (e.g., "BTC ETF inflows surge", "Memecoin rotation on Solana").
- **Alpha Calls** — Specific token mentions from high-signal accounts.
- **Whale Movements** — Notable on-chain transfers and accumulation.
- **On-Chain Signals** — Liquidations, funding-rate flips, unusual volume.
- **Degen Plays** — Early-stage tokens picking up rapid attention.

## Schedule Your Daily Briefing

Don't want to even open the app? You can schedule Market Pulse to be delivered to your **email** or **Telegram** every morning. Wake up, read your digest over coffee, and know exactly where the market stands.

<Tip>
  Scheduled Market Pulse executions are fixed at **70 credits** for a 24h window or **40 credits** for a 12h window — see [Credits](/credits) for details.
</Tip>
